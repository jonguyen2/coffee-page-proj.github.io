CAFES

A bad day with coffee is better than a good day without it.


Antidote Coffee

729 Studewood St Houston, TX 77007
(713) 861-7400

This low-key establishment features pastries, beer, wine and coffees in an unassuming environment with outdoor/indoor seating and free WiFi, ideal for studying and reading.

Visit Website

Boomtown Coffee

242 W 19th St Houston, TX 77008
(713) 862-7018

Matthew Toomey’s quintessential Heights coffee shop is the archetype for fellow craft coffee shops in Houston when it comes to roasting artisanal coffee in small batches for the best brew.

Visit Website

Catalina Coffee

2201 Washington Ave Houston, TX 77007
(713) 861-8448

This cozy spot is a popular destination for laptop warriors, so getting there early to snag a seat is a must. Once settled in, enjoy a pastry from Weights + Measures and sip a perfectly-decorated latte.

Visit Website

Inversion Coffee

1953 Montrose Blvd Houston, TX 77006
(713) 523-4866

Another Montrose favorite for the artistic-chic study set, Inversion’s loyal crowd enjoys the creative coffee concoctions, relaxed vibe and sense of community. Try a frothy chai cream, honey badger (steamed milk, espresso, honey and cinnamon) or latte.

Visit Website

Morning Star

4721 N Main St Ste M Houston, TX 77009
(832) 382-3466

Outside of the stellar doughnuts and breakfast dishes, the caffeinated offerings at David Buehrer's Morningstar are as solid as you might expect. Order a cortado or latte, then dive into one of the neighborhood's best brunches.

Visit Website

Paper Co Cafe

1100 Elder St Houston, TX 77007
(713) 522-3533

This Downtown coffee shop gets its name from the refurbished paper factory where it is now housed. Sip on a café au lait and nosh on Kaya French toast (Texas toast with coconut custard and spiced almonds) while taking a moment to admire the vaulted ceilings.

Visit Website

Southside Expresso Coffee

904 Westheimer Rd Houston, TX 77006
(713) 942-9990

Located on some primo real estate beside Uchi, Southside Espresso offers innovative coffee creations in a quiet setting. Parking and seating is limited.

Visit Website

Tout Suite

2001 Commerce St Houston, TX 77002
(713) 277-8688

Sip an Americano, drip coffee, or latte in this breezy industrial chic space, and plan to stay a while. The large tables, chill vibes, and strong coffee make for a solid space to get work done or spend a Saturday.

Visit Website

Blacksmith

1018 Westheimer Rd Houston, TX 77006
(832) 360-7470

Blacksmith is a Montrose cult cafe that churns out some seriously perfect roasts from Greenway Coffee's David Buehrer and his merry band of skilled baristas. Sit down with a creamy flat white.

Visit Website
